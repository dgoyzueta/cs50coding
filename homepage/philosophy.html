I have reflected a lot about my experience and ideas taken from many courses and books:

The world and the economy are uncertain, so taking action, advancing yourself, and

building on the go is what we should keep doing all the time. We should try ideas and not

be afraid of challenging conventional ways of doing things.


I like this quote from George Bernard Shaw:

"The reasonable man adapts himself to the world: the unreasonable one persists in trying

to adapt the world to himself. Therefore all progress depends on the unreasonable man."
